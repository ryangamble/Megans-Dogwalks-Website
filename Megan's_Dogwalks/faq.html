FAQ

Q: Do we offer dog walking services on the weekend?

A: Yes, we offer all services every day of the week.

Q: What happens if my dog gets sick or injured under your care?

A: If your dog is sick or injured during a dog walk or dog sit, the walker/sitter will contact you and bring the dog home immediately if it is not already. If your dog is sick or injured during a boarding stay, the dog will be tended to by our on staff vet until you can arrive to pick up the dog.

Q: Do I have to be home for a walker to come walk my dog?

A: No, you are not required to be home if you leave instructions/keys for the walker to access the dog.

Q: What if I want to board my dog but I don't want it to be with other dogs?

A: We offer private boarding options where you dog will remain separate from any other dogs.
