The Doggybox

What is it?

The Doggybox is a monthly subscription service where we send you everything you need to take care of your dog so that you never have to make a last minute trip to the store for pet supplies ever again.

What's included?

In your Doggybox, you will receive:

  • A bag of dog treats
  • A new, exclusive dog collar
  • 3 new dog toys
  • A bottle of dog shampoo
  • A roll of dog waste bags

Call now to customize and order your first Doggybox!*

*Price varies and depends on package selected. Speak to one of our Doggybox reps to learn more.
