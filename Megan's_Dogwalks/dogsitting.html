Dog Sitting Service

Helping You Take Care of What's Important

Here at Megan's Dogwalks, we want nothing more than for you and your dog to have the best experience possible. That's why we have started offering a convenient dog-sitting service. Now it doesn't matter if you have to work late or take a trip for a couple days, you will never have to worry about your dog being lonely again.

Don't believe us? Take a minute to read this article and see all the benefits of using a dogsitter.

How Much it Costs

  • For visits under 2 hours: $20/hour.
  • For visits over 2 hours: $17.50/hour

How it Works

Just give your dog-sitter access to your home (using our key lock-box, key under the mat, etc.) and the sitter will take care of your dog and leave things as instructed. (key on the hook, back under the mat, etc.).
