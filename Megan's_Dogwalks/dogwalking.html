Dog Walking Service

How We Make Your Life Easier

Our number one goal is to make your life easier, that is why we made it easier than ever to book a dog-walk. Using our service, you can book a walk at any time of the day, and you don't even have to be home for the dog-walker to walk your dog. Just leave the provided padlock key box on the front door and our dog-walker can use it to open your door and access your dog.

Need a couple more reasons to start using a dogwalker? Take a look at what this article has to say about using a dogwalker.

How Much it Costs

We offer two walk lengths as follows:

  • 30 minutes: $20
  • 1 hour: $37.50

Customers who choose to use a padlock key box will pay a $40 refundable deposit for the equipment.

Our dog-walkers are paid by the hour and do not receive tips.

How it Works

Just use our app or give us a call to book a walk and a dog-walker will be by to walk your dog.
