Lebanon Boarding Location

About the Location

Our Lebanon location features a large enclosed countryside property for your dog to roam and explore with other dogs or by itself. This location has plenty of small animal wildlife to keep your dog entertained and a small creek for your dog to take a refreshing dip in on hot days. This is the best location for dogs who are used to being outdoors and enjoy nature.

Cost of boarding

Boarding costs are determined on a case by case basis but generally prices are as follows:

  • Daily fee: $50
  • Food fee:
    • Large dogs: $15/day
    • Medium dogs: $10/day
    • Small dogs: $5/day
  • Medicine fee: varies*

*Talk to a boarding rep to determine fees for medication administration while boarding

How to Board your Dog

Our boarding process is the same for both locations:

  1. Call to reserve a spot
  2. Drop off your dog at the agreed time at our Lebanon boarding location
  3. Pay fees for the visit
  4. Pick up your dog at the agreed time at our Lebanon boarding location
