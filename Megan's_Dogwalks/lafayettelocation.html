Lafayette Boarding Location

About the Location

Our Lafayette location includes large fields of green grass for your dog to run around in as well as a dog park for visitors to bring their dogs to play. Since launching this has been our most popular location with owners bringing their dogs by for the dog park daily. Of course in addition to the public access dog park is our private boarding service.

Cost of boarding

Boarding costs are determined on a case by case basis but generally prices are as follows:

  • Daily fee: $50
  • Food fee:
    • Large dogs: $15/day
    • Medium dogs: $10/day
    • Small dogs: $5/day
  • Medicine fee: varies*

*Talk to a boarding rep to determine fees for medication administration while boarding

How to Board your Dog

Our boarding process is the same for both locations:

  1. Call to reserve a spot
  2. Drop off your dog at the agreed time at our Lafayette boarding location
  3. Pay fees for the visit
  4. Pick up your dog at the agreed time at our Lafayette boarding location
