Dog Training Service

Why Train With Us?

We have a proven track record of training a wide variety of dogs and all of our trainers are certified and well trained. Don't deal with the trouble of trying to train your dog and getting nowhere. Make training a breeze by calling today.

Still not convinced? Take a look at this article on the importance of dog training.

How Much it Costs

We have a variety of training classes available as follows:

  • Bathroom: $100 (Trains your dog not to go to the bathroom indoors)
  • Obedience: $200 (Covers barking, chewing, biting, general behavior)
  • Tricks 1: $200 (Trains your dog to sit, stay, and shake)
  • Tricks 2: $300 (Trains your dog to spin, rollover, and stand up)

How it Works

You can either bring your dog to our facility* or a trainer can visit your home for a training session.

*(Bathroom training is at customer home only)
